Discover our new Project

Play the KRYPTO_HUNT® Game

This game targets students from Centrale Marseille as well as Kedge Business School, two schools in which the KRYPTOSPHERE® project is present.
The goal of this game is very simple: work as a team to hunt for crypto-assets (ethers) in the heart of Marseille - where QR Codes to be scanned will be placed in various emblematic places of the city - and earn money in a fun, secure and automated way thanks to Blockchain technology.

Our Business Partner

This game is organised in participation with the students KRYPTOSPHERE® team of Kedge Business School. They take care of the event communication as well as finding partnership to finance this project.


Our Technical Partner

The technical side of this event is managed by our engeenering students of Centrale Marseille. They take care of the frond end and back end solutions, to create a user-friendly game everyone can play!

Our Cultural Partner

The organising of the cultural aspect of this game is made by the Cultural Centrale Marseille society. From the places chosen to the disposition of the QR Codes, they will make you discover the city like never before.
